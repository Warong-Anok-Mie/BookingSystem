About Us

Warong Anok Mie is a restaurant that opened in 2019 and is situated in Chendering, Kuala Terengganu. Founded by Nur Amiera Binti Mat Amin, the sole ownership of the Warong Anok Mie, she first started the business by selling small drinks outside her home, and later on, she added a variety of thai foods like Maggie Kerabu, which later became the signature dish of the establishment. After receiving a lot of positive feedbacks from customers and amassing a huge amount of profit, she started renting on a shoplot near her home and expanded th business by introducing more menus such as western and more.
