About Us

Who We Are

Warong Anok Mie is dedicated to bringing you the best local flavors with a modern twist. Our team of skilled chefs uses only the freshest ingredients to create mouth-watering dishes that will keep you coming back for more.

Established in 2021, Warong Anok Mie has quickly become a favorite dining spot for locals and tourists alike. We take pride in our friendly service, cozy ambiance, and a menu that offers something for everyone.

We offer a wide variety of dishes, from traditional local favorites to innovative new creations that blend the best of local and international cuisine. Our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience, whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special occasion.
